Privacy Policy

PicToCards · Effective date: June 11, 2026

Short version: PicToCards does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. Everything you create stays on your device.

PicToCards (“the App”) is a language-learning app developed by Anastasiia Kharicheva (“we,” “us,” or “our”). This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles your information. By using the App, you agree to this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. The App contains no analytics, advertising, tracking, or crash-reporting tools, and we operate no servers. We never receive, see, or store any information about you or how you use the App.

2. Content You Create

Any cards, decks, images, and game progress you create are stored locally on your device only. This content is never transmitted to us or to any third party. If you delete the App, this content is removed along with it. Device-level backups (for example, iCloud or computer backups) are managed entirely by you and by Apple's operating system, and are outside our control; please refer to Apple's policies for how those backups are handled.

3. Purchases and Subscriptions

The App offers auto-renewable subscriptions. All purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive or store your payment details or Apple ID information. Apple's handling of your data is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

4. Children's Privacy

PicToCards is an educational app suitable for all ages, including children. Because the App collects no personal information from anyone, it collects none from children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your country or region).

5. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party SDKs or services, other than Apple's App Store, which is used solely to process subscriptions as described above.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised effective date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us at: picktocardsapp@gmail.com.
